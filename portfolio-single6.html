Scientia: Empowering Minds with the Latest Economic Insights

Overview

Scientia is a platform that was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to make cutting-edge research and in-depth analysis accessible to everyone. Whether you're a researcher, a business professional, or a student, Scientia provides the information you need to stay on top of economic trends and developments. We’re filling a void left by outdated and hard-to-access platforms, ensuring that people can make informed decisions with confidence.

Client

Sterling Bank

Year

2020-present

Category

SAAS

The Problem We Saw

Current State of the Publishing World

Today’s publishing industry is great at churning out news and categorizing content, but it’s falling short when it comes to delivering the kind of deep, data-backed information that really matters. The result? A lot of people are left digging through irrelevant or outdated content, struggling to find the information they need.

The Gap We Wanted to Fill

We realized there wasn’t a single platform offering comprehensive, up-to-date data and the context needed to make sense of it. So, we decided to create one.

Our Solution

Scientia is our answer. We didn’t just want to throw data at people—we wanted to make it meaningful. That’s why we offer downloadable data files that anyone can use, along with podcasts that break down and explain the numbers, making the information more digestible and useful. We’re here to help people not just access information but understand it.

Who We’re Helping

Scientia is for:

  1. Researchers: Who need reliable, up-to-the-minute data for their work.
  2. Business Professionals: Who require insights to guide their decisions.
  3. Students: Who need solid sources for their studies.

Our goal is to give everyone, from individuals to organizations, the tools and knowledge they need to succeed.

Our Design Journey

Understanding the People We Serve:

We started by talking to the people who would use Scientia—researchers, business professionals, and students. We wanted to know their pain points and what they were missing from other platforms. Inspired by McKinsey’s Featured Insights, we aimed to do more by providing current, accessible data and content that explains complex topics in an easy-to-understand way.

Brainstorming and Creating

Armed with this understanding, we brainstormed ideas and sketched out designs, keeping user feedback at the forefront of our process. We weren’t just creating a platform; we were building a tool that people could rely on.

Bringing the Vision to Life:

We translated our ideas into wireframes and high-fidelity mockups, refining them through multiple rounds of usability testing. Collaborating with a graphic designer, we developed a visual identity that’s professional yet approachable, with a logo and color scheme that reflect our mission.

Working Hand-in-Hand with Developers

To bring Scientia to life, we worked closely with our developers, ensuring that every design element was implemented seamlessly. Using agile methodologies, we tackled challenges head-on and made sure that the transition from design to development was smooth and efficient.

Behind the Scenes: Making It All Work (Admin Dashboard)

We designed the admin dashboard to be the command center of the platform. It gives a clear overview of everything happening on Scientia—from post-performance to user engagement, all in one easy-to-use interface.

Creating and Managing Content

Admins can effortlessly create and manage posts, categorizing them for easy access and adding multimedia elements to enhance the content. Whether it's scheduling posts or making quick edits, the process is straightforward and efficient.

Keeping Things Organized

Managing categories is simple, ensuring that all content is organized logically and is easy for users to find. As the platform grows, these categories can be updated to reflect new content strategies.

Results and Metrics


User Engagement

Since going live, the platform has seen a high rate in user registrations and data downloads. These metrics reflect the platform's growing impact and its ability to meet the needs of its users.

Business Impact

Testimonials from users indicate that businesses and individuals find the platform invaluable for staying informed and making data-driven decisions. The ability to access reliable, up-to-date information has empowered users to navigate the complexities of the economic landscape more effectively.

Feedback and Continuous Improvement


User Feedback

Our users have praised Scientia for the comprehensiveness and accessibility of its data. However, they’ve also expressed a desire for more interactive features that would allow them to engage with the content on a deeper level.

Ongoing Modifications

In response to this feedback, we’re currently working on adding interactive data visualization tools and expanding the podcast library. Future enhancements include the development of a mobile app and personalized user dashboards to further enrich the user experience.

Challenges


Market Research

One of the key challenges we faced was conducting market research concurrently with the design development. This required frequent meetings and redesigns, but the iterative approach ensured that the final product was finely tuned to meet user needs effectively. This collaboration between research and design was crucial to the success of the platform.
